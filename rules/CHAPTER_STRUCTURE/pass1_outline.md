<output\_structure>

\## Chapter \[NUMBER] Outline



\### Opening Hook

\[Specific action or image that grounds the reader immediately]



\### Scene Breakdown

For each scene, provide:

1\. \*\*Location \& Time\*\*: Where and when

2\. \*\*POV Character's Goal\*\*: What they want in this scene

3\. \*\*Obstacle\*\*: What prevents them from getting it

4\. \*\*Turning Point\*\*: The moment that changes the scene's direction

5\. \*\*Scene Outcome\*\*: How it ends (yes-but / no-and)

6\. \*\*Information Revealed\*\*: What the reader learns (plot, character, world)



\### Chapter Arc

\- \*\*Opening State\*\*: Character's emotional/physical situation at start

\- \*\*Escalation Points\*\*: 2-3 moments of rising tension

\- \*\*Chapter Climax\*\*: The peak moment

\- \*\*Closing Hook\*\*: What propels reader to next chapter



\### Threads Advanced

\- \[ ] Main plot thread moved forward by: \[SPECIFIC BEAT]

\- \[ ] Character development shown through: \[SPECIFIC ACTION/CHOICE]

\- \[ ] World-building integrated via: \[SPECIFIC DETAIL]

\- \[ ] Foreshadowing planted for: \[FUTURE EVENT]



\### Cruel Irony Check

Does this chapter contain dramatic irony where characters possess information but cannot act on it? If not, identify opportunity to add this.
