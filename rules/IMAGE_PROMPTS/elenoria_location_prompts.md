\# Elenoria Location Prompts

\## Key Settings in Aelindril and the Eternal Woods



---



\## Visual Consistency - All Locations



| Element | Standard |

|---------|----------|

| \*\*Lighting\*\* | Golden sunlight through leaves, silver starlight, soft diffused glow - ALWAYS natural |

| \*\*Architecture\*\* | Grown from living wood, organic curves, no harsh angles, Art Nouveau feel |

| \*\*Materials\*\* | Living wood, silver metal, crystal, moonlight fabric, natural stone |

| \*\*Colors\*\* | Emerald green, silver-white, gold, sapphire blue, amber |

| \*\*Atmosphere\*\* | Serene, ancient, wise, subtly otherworldly |

| \*\*Nature\*\* | Integrated everywhere - structures grow from trees, vines on everything |

\## 1. The Starlight Throne



\*\*Location:\*\* Crown Canopy of Aeltharis

\*\*Purpose:\*\* Royal court, governance, formal audiences

\*\*Story Connection:\*\* Where outsiders would petition for help



\*\*Visual Elements:\*\*

\- Open-air throne platform 400 feet up

\- Throne grown from living silver-barked wood

\- Canopy of silver leaves overhead, glowing softly

\- Living bridges connecting to other platforms

\- View of all seven great trees

\- Floating lights like captured stars

\- Other platforms with courtiers visible

\- Wind moving through the space



\*\*Atmosphere:\*\* Ancient authority, serene power, timeless governance



\*\*Prompt:\*\*

```

Photorealistic interior of the Starlight Throne, high elf royal court in the crown canopy of a colossal ancient tree. Open-air platform 400 feet above the forest floor. Throne grown from living silver-barked wood, its branches forming a natural canopy above the monarch's seat. Overhead, silver leaves that glow with soft inner light even in daytime. Living bridges of white wood span to other visible platforms where courtiers stand. View through gaps shows all seven great trees of the elven city. Floating lights like captured stars drift through the space. Elegant elven nobles in flowing robes visible on surrounding platforms. A gentle wind moves through, stirring hair and fabric. The throne faces east toward the rising sun. Atmosphere of ancient serene authority. Golden sunlight filtering through silver leaves creating dappled patterns. Shot with Phase One IQ4 150MP, 24mm f/4, wide angle capturing the majesty of the space.

```

\- Memory pools reflecting recorded images

\- Scholars at desks of living wood

\*\*Atmosphere:\*\* Accumulated wisdom, patient study, protected knowledge

```

\- Flowering vines

\- Walking elves crossing

\- Impossible grace



\*\*Atmosphere:\*\* Elven achievement, defying nature through harmony

```

Photorealistic scene of Star Fall Bridge, the longest living bridge in the high elf city. Spanning 400 feet between the two highest great trees at the crown canopy level. Through gaps in the bridge floor, stars are visible even during daylight - some enchantment or dimensional quirk. Flowering vines grow along the railings, purple and white blossoms.
