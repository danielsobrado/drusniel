\## Style Guidelines



\### Consistent Elements

\- \*\*Drow appearance:\*\* Dark obsidian/charcoal skin, white hair, pointed ears, red or violet eyes

\- \*\*Lighting:\*\* Bioluminescent fungi (blue-purple), purple flames, no natural sunlight

\- \*\*Architecture:\*\* Carved black stone, spider motifs, elegant-menacing blend

\- \*\*Color palette:\*\* Blacks, deep purples, silver accents, violet magical effects

\- \*\*Atmosphere:\*\* Underground, vast caverns, political tension



\### Art Style Notes

\- Fantasy illustration style (not photorealistic)

\- High detail and dramatic lighting

\- Emotional resonance appropriate to scene

\- Avoid: Bright colors, sunny lighting, smiling faces (unless contextually appropriate)



\### Recommended AI Image Tools

\- Midjourney (best for fantasy atmosphere)

\- DALL-E 3 (good for specific details)

\- Stable Diffusion (with fantasy-trained models)
