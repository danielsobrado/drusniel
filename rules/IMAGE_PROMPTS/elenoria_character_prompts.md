\# Elenoria Character Portrait Prompts

\## High Elf Social Hierarchy - Photorealistic Generation



---



\## Visual Consistency - All High Elves



| Feature | Standard |

|---------|----------|

| \*\*Skin\*\* | Pale luminous, ranges from porcelain to warm ivory, subtle golden undertones |

| \*\*Hair\*\* | Silver, gold, platinum blonde, copper, deep brown - never black |

| \*\*Eyes\*\* | Silver, gold, deep blue, violet, emerald - luminous, ancient |

| \*\*Build\*\* | Tall (6-7 feet), slender, graceful, deceptively strong |

| \*\*Ears\*\* | Long, pointed, elegant - 4-6 inches |

| \*\*Age Signs\*\* | Only the very ancient show age - silver at temples, deeper wisdom in eyes |

| \*\*Movement\*\* | Implied grace even in stillness |

| \*\*Expression\*\* | Serene, knowing, patient - rarely show strong emotion |



---



\## Lighting Standard



All Elenoria portraits should feature:

\- Golden sunlight filtering through leaves OR silver starlight

\- Dappled natural light patterns

\- Soft, diffused illumination

\- Green/gold color temperature

\- No harsh shadows

\- Subtle embroidery of star patterns

\- Throne grown from living silver-barked tree

\- Canopy of glowing leaves overhead

\- Starlight filtering through even in daytime

\- Other platforms visible in distance

\- Wind gently moving hair and leaves
