# BLACK CRYSTALS — CANON BIBLE (LOCKED)

## Core Function

Black crystals do NOT increase magical power.

They temporarily reconfigure the user’s physical and magical tolerance, allowing stable action in environments where magic, pressure, heat, and visibility are structurally incompatible with normal bodies.

They reduce friction.
They do not add force.

---

## What the Crystals Do

- Stabilize spell formation under hostile magical conditions
- Reduce sensory overload (heat, pressure, distortion)
- Allow precise casting where magic normally resists shaping
- Temporarily normalize environments that should feel unbearable

---

## What the Crystals Do NOT Do

- Do NOT grant new abilities
- Do NOT amplify raw magical output
- Do NOT bypass Nexus artifact hierarchy
- Do NOT override choice or intent
- Do NOT function outside hostile environments

A crystal used in a neutral environment provides little to no benefit.

---

## Cost (Delayed, Not Immediate)

Repeated crystal use causes the body to:

- Recalibrate what it considers “normal” magical conditions
- Lose sensitivity to environmental warning signs
- Become increasingly compatible with structurally hostile magic

This adaptation is gradual and largely unnoticed by the user.

---

## Narrative Purpose

Black crystals exist to ensure:

- Survival choices feel rational and responsible
- Long-term catastrophe is enabled without coercion
- “Why Drusniel?” is answered biologically, not prophetically
- Final barrier interaction feels inevitable, not forced

They are not corruptive.
They are preparatory.

---

## Political Control (Nyxara)

Nyxara controls black crystals because uncontrolled compatibility destabilizes territorial and magical governance.

Adapted individuals:
- Ignore environmental deterrents
- Bypass territorial constraints
- Become system-level threats without appearing powerful

Crystals are regulated to prevent systemic collapse, not hoarded for dominance.

---

## Prose Execution Rules (HARD)

- Never describe crystal use as empowering
- Never frame adaptation as mastery
- Never allow a crystal to solve a problem cleanly
- At least once, crystal use must succeed and still cause loss
- Body changes must introduce friction, not advantage

If a scene makes crystal use feel optimal without consequence, revise.

---

## Final Canon Statement

Black crystals do not make someone strong enough to break the world.

They make someone fit well enough that the world stops resisting them.
